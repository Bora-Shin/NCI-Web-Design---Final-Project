Ridora is a new premium Blended Irish Whiskey, named in honour of Roeder Gabo, Rich Sun and Bora Shin,
the true pioneers of Irish Whiskey from Philippines, China and Korea.

made from the green meadow with the hand-picked stocks of irish malt and grain whiskies by three international students, aged in bourbon casks.

NOSE

Creamy cinamon fragrant.

TASTE

Soft spice and caramelized sugar along with cozy hints of woody vanilla.

FINISH

Gentle creaminess with the lingering scent of Christmas.

130cl / 45% ABV

20 years of experience

Roeder, Bora and Rich are three Asian immigrants in ireland who created a luxury premium whiskey.Ridora boasts a perfect harmony between the explicit fruitiness of the malt and the soft creaminess of the grain whiskeys.
Ridora is non-chill filtered and bottled at a higher than usual ABV of 45%.
